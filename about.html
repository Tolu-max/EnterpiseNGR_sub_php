About
Us
An economy with exponential potential.
We are an independent member-led group representing the Nigerian financial and professional services (FPS) sector.
Our goal is to support the growth and development of the Nigerian financial and professional services sector, the economy, and our members.

Nigeria’s financial and professional services (FPS) sector contributes majorly to the nation’s economic growth and hence is crucial to enabling the positive development of the economy. Yet, amongst the many subsectors within Nigeria’s FPS, only the banking subsector has made giant strides in the last few decades achieving about 60% penetration in Nigeria with strong African footprints and international presence in key financial markets.


This growth needs to be sustained and replicated across other subsectors if we hope to advance the impact of the FPS, greatly strengthen the economy, and position the sector as a prime financial center for Africa. To do this, the sector needs an enabling and stable policy environment built, grown, and maintained by deliberate effort.


This is where EnterpriseNGR comes in.

Nurturing an enabling environment for engagement.
EnterpriseNGR nurtures an enabling environment for engagement with policymakers and champions business sector advocacy domestically and internationally. This is while working together with government authorities and key stakeholders to achieve positive outcomes that would lead to economic growth and development.
Supporting the formation of policies
As a consolidated advocacy group, we support government and regulators in the formation of policies that improve the financial inclusion and performance of FPS sector operators in Nigeria. These policies will result in increased tax receipts for the government, increased credit to the real sector, and overall increased employment opportunities. We take pride in doing this while protecting the interest of our members.
OUR HISTORY
How We Started

EnterpriseNGR was launched in October 2021 as an independent professional policy and advocacy group with the aim of promoting and advocating for the Financial and Professional Services (FPS) sector of Nigeria.


Founded by some of the sector's key players, our focus is to transform Nigeria into the premier financial services center in Africa, improve financial literacy and inclusion, and significantly impact the quality of life of Nigerians.


We leverage the expertise of our members who are industry professionals cutting across various subsectors of the Nigerian FPS sector, to tap the country’s teeming economic potential in a bid to develop a strong and resilient economy.

Governance
Members of our Advisory Board consists of a high-level representation of industry leaders, and relevant Government and Regulatory institutions.
These include the Central Bank of Nigeria, Ministry of Finance, Leadership of relevant House Committees, National Pension Commission, Securities and Exchange Commission, National Insurance Commission, etc.
Some examples of committees where our members are involved are:
  • International Trade Committee
  • G.10 Alliance (Exclusive Club of Top players in each subsector)
  • G.10 Alliance (Exclusive Club of Top players in each subsector)
