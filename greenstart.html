Taming Unemployment
At EnterpriseNGR, we decided to not just point out the problem of unemployment, but to bring a solution to it. That is why we created the Youths of Enterprise (YOE) Internship Programme.
Youths of Enterprise (YOE) Internship Programme is one of EnterpriseNGR’s immediate developmental initiatives that aims to reverse the national youth unemployment and under-employment trends as well as build capacity in the private sector.
The National Population Commission places Nigeria’s population at about 220 million with 50% of the population comprising youths. Unfortunately, over 38.5% of Nigerian youths remain unemployed and a larger percentage are underemployed.
We seek to improve the employment and employability of Nigerian youth within the Financial and Professional Services (“FPS”) sector with the YOE Internship Programme.

Our Approach
Through the YOE Programme, EnterpriseNGR is playing a vital role in facilitating capacity building in the private sector and contributing to resolving the problem of unemployment in Nigeria.
Working with identified partners in the financial and professional services sector, we will engage and recruit about 5,000 interns who are graduates with not more than five years post-graduation for placements to partner organizations.
The Programme is designed to improve the skillsets, professional competence, and employability of program participants while also providing them with workplace experience.
Following the envisaged success of the program, we aim to scale to 100,000 annually in five years
OUR MANIFESTO
YOE is EnterpriseNGR’s commitment to:
  • Empower the next generation of workplace talent
  • Create meaningful work experiences.
  • Foster an Ecosystem of support and networking.
  • Curate opportunities for our interns.
  • Deepen our community enterprise footprint.
